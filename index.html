Tip Calculator

A Tip Calculator is a handy tool designed to calculate the total tip amount based on a bill. Users simply input the bill amount and the percentage they wish to give as a tip. The calculator then computes the tip value and displays the total amount to be paid, including the original bill and the tip. It’s a quick and efficient way to ensure accurate tipping, often used in restaurants, cafes, or other service scenarios.
